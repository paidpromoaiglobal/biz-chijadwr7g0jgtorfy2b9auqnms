Our Story

Made with Passion

Truptee Legacy is Patia's most beloved pure vegetarian restaurant, nestled near Infocity Square in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. With a warm ambience, affordable prices, and a menu that spans South Indian breakfast classics to North Indian curries and Indo-Chinese specialties, we have been crafting memorable dining experiences for our guests every single day from 7 AM to 10 PM.

Rated 4.2 out of 5 by over 1,300 happy diners, Truptee Legacy takes pride in offering a diverse, 100% vegetarian menu that caters to every palate. Whether you are stopping by for a soft idli and filter coffee in the morning or indulging in Paneer Singapore, Nan Mushroom Hyderabadi, or Malai Kofta for lunch and dinner, every dish is prepared with care and authentic flavours that define true culinary legacy.
